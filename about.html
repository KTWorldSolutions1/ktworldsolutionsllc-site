About KT World Solutions

Built to serve logistics, healthcare support, infrastructure, and community care.

Company Overview

KT World Solutions LLC is a parent company with multiple operational lanes.

KT World Solutions LLC supports transportation, secure courier services, truck parking development, and assisted living development. The company is built around dependable service, clear communication, and a practical approach to solving real operational needs.

Based in Greensboro, North Carolina, KT World Solutions is positioned to serve clients, partners, and communities across North Carolina and the Southeast.
