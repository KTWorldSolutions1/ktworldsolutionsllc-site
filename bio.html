Founder Bio

Travis Lewter

Founder & Chief Executive Officer of KT World Solutions LLC.

Founder • CEO • Owner

Leadership with real operating experience.

Travis Lewter is the Founder and Chief Executive Officer of KT World Solutions LLC, a diversified operations company serving North Carolina and the Southeast through transportation, medical courier services, truck parking development, and assisted living initiatives.

With hands-on experience in CDL transportation, logistics operations, employee development, leadership, and business growth, Travis built KT World Solutions to operate with reliability, safety, professionalism, and long-term community impact.

His vision is to create a company that does more than provide services. KT World Solutions is being built as a platform for dependable operations, strategic partnerships, job creation, and practical solutions across multiple industries.
